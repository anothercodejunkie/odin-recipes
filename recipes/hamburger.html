How to Make a Hamburger

A Classic Burger

This is a simple recipe for a classic hamburger or cheeseburger.

Ingredients

Required:

  • Ground beef
  • Bun

Optional

  • Cheese (optional)
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Mustard
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Put on bread
  3. Add cheese if wanted
  4. Add other toppings
  5. Eat!
